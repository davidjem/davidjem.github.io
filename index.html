Car Fixes

Garage Bodywork and paint

I was curious to learn how to paint a car, so I decided to try on my own truck. Using stud welding, I pulled the big dents, then applied paint with a small air compressor and budget paint gun.
During the process, I accidentally used the wrong reducer and had to sand down part of the base coat. Despite this setback, the final result was excellent for a garage paint job!

Resurrecting an abandoned truck

I purchased this 1978 Chevy after sitting for more than 10 years abandoned, and I was determined to bring it back to life. After some hours tinkering with it I was able to get it running and driving again.

Electrical Fixes in a Car

After chasing down an alternator light issue for months. I traced the wiring and found a broken wire. I repaired the wire and the alternator light issue was resolved.
