This are some of the projects I have done in my free time.

Cane holder for grandma

My wife's grandma was having a hard time using her walker while holding her cane. I decided to design a holder that was intuitive and she could use without any help. I designed it in SolidWorks, 3d printed it at home, and assemble it myself.

Restoring and optimizing a 3d printer

With no prior experience in 3d printing, I purchased a used non-functional 3d printer and succesfully restored it to working condition. This project involved the diagnosis of mechanical and electrical components of the printer. I diagnosed and resolved several issues, including reversed fan cables, a cracked Bowden tube holder at the extruder, an unlevel bed, and a clogged nozzle.
